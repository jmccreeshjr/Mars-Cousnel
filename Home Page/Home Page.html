Who is Mars Counsel?

Mars Counsel is a Philadelphia-based project consisting of multi-instrumental artists, Kaile Martinelli and James Kull. Having backgrounds in instrumental guitar (James) and soulful/theatrical vocals (Kaile), the duo nurtures the fusion of Americana, Indie and Blues. Deeply influenced by artists like Frank Ocean, Hozier, and Rainbow Kitten Surprise, Mars Counsel creates with a fine-tuned appreciation for lyrical and compositional detail allowing for a dynamic/captivating sound.
